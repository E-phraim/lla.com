Grassroots Tech Academy for Kids: Fostering Future-Ready Skills

Our Grassroots Tech Academy provides a unique approach to secondary education, offering young students the chance to build technological skills at a young age. Our focus is on restructuring learning to instill future-ready skills, preparing kids for the demands of tomorrow's workforce. Join us in fostering a love of technology and empowering the next generation.

Nurturing Tech Prodigies: The Tech Generations Program

The Tech Generations program is dedicated to identifying and nurturing the tech leaders of tomorrow. Each month, we select 10 exceptional young individuals and provide them with the resources and support they need to excel. Our intensive cohort program supports 120 kids, giving them the tools and guidance they need to become the tech innovators of the future.

STEM Practical

Our innovative approach to science education harnesses the power of Augmented Reality (AR) to bring scientific concepts to life. By using AR to illustrate scientific practicals, we engage students in a more immersive and interactive learning experience. With our cutting-edge technology, students can explore scientific phenomena and conduct experiments in a safe and engaging virtual environment. Join us in revolutionizing science education and preparing students for the challenges of the future.
